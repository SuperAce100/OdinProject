Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Indulge in the ultimate frozen delight: homemade vanilla ice cream. This classic recipe combines velvety cream, whole milk, and a dash of magic. With each spoonful, you'll experience the rich essence of vanilla and a creamy texture that's simply irresistible. Create your own frozen masterpiece and treat yourself to a scoop of pure happiness. Your taste buds will thank you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream and milk. Place the saucepan over medium heat and heat the mixture until it reaches a gentle simmer. Do not let it boil.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, egg yolks, and salt until well combined.
  3. Gradually pour about 1/2 cup of the hot cream mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly. This will temper the eggs and prevent them from curdling.
  4. Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cream mixture. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon or spatula, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of the spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes. Be careful not to let the mixture boil.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature. Then, cover the saucepan and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to chill thoroughly.
  6. Once the mixture is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  7. Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.
  8. Serve the homemade vanilla ice cream in bowls or cones and enjoy!

You can also customize your vanilla ice cream by adding mix-ins such as chocolate chips, crushed cookies, or fruit during the last few minutes of churning. Enjoy your delicious homemade vanilla ice cream!
